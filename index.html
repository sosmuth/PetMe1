FAQ

Visit the site and look for Become a Caretaker button in the navigation bar at the top. From there on you could register or log in. Register, then login if you are a new user. You shall see a confirmation message pop at the top of the page. Click on Close.

In the navigation bar at the top hover over the person icon, click on it, then select account. Further if you are a new user the login just after you register. For already registered users, just select login, enter the credentials and you'd be in. You shall see a confirmation message pop at the top of the page. Click on Close.

When there are several registered profiles of both caretakers and pet owners, a pet owner can log into their account. After that, upon scrolling down they could see a display of all the caretakers registered on this platform. They select a particular caretaker profile and fill a request form with the necessary details. A request mail would be sent to that particular caretaker. They could accept the request by clicking on accept or else decline the request. A status mail shall be received by the pet Owner.
